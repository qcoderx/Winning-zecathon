1. Introduction &amp; Objective
Product Objective: To build a functional Minimum Viable Product (MVP) for the Zenith Bank Zecathon 5.0 finals. This MVP will demonstrate PulseFi&#39;s unique capability to solve the &quot;Billion-Naira Trust Gap&quot; by programmatically verifying SME authenticity and financial health , thereby de-risking SME lending.
The Problem: Lenders are paralyzed by the high risk and inefficiency of verifying SMEs. Credible SMEs are, in turn, starved of capital because they cannot prove their viability. The core issue is a lack of trusted, verifiable data.
The Solution (Our MVP): An intelligent platform that implements a &quot;Twin Score System&quot;.
The Pulse Score validates SME authenticity.
The Profit Score analyzes the financial health of verified businesses.
This feeds a Marketplace where lenders can confidently find and fund pre-vetted SMEs.
2. User Personas
For the MVP, we will focus on two primary personas:
Sade (The SME Owner):
Needs: Access to formal financing to grow her business.
Pains: Is &quot;invisible&quot; to lenders. She is frustrated by her inability to demonstrate her business&#39;s credibility and health.
Goal: To get a verifiable &quot;financial passport&quot; that proves her business is legitimate and worthy of investment.
Bayo (The Lender/Investor):
Needs: A pipeline of high-potential, authentic SMEs to invest in.
Pains: High risk of fraudulent applications. Manual verification is slow, expensive, and doesn&#39;t scale. He is cautious due to fear of loss.
Goal: To make simple, safe, and data-driven investment decisions based on undeniable proof.
3. MVP Feature Set &amp; User Stories
This is the core build. We must demonstrate the end-to-end flow from SME verification to lender discovery.
Epic 1: SME Onboarding &amp; Pulse Score Generation (The &quot;Authenticity Gate&quot;)
User Story 1.1 (Sade): As Sade, I must be able to sign up and create a business profile by filling out a form with my operational and financial information (the &quot;Stated Truth&quot;). This form will require me to upload my CAC certificate as a PDF or image.
User Story 1.2 (Sade): As Sade, I must be prompted to record a 30-60 second live video of my business location and operations using my phone&#39;s camera (the &quot;Visual Truth&quot;). The system must ensure this video cannot be edited or uploaded from a file.
User Story 1.3 (Sade): As Sade, I must be able to securely connect my business bank account via the Mono API (the &quot;Financial Truth&quot;).
User Story 1.4 (System): As the PulseFi AI, I must receive the &quot;Stated,&quot; &quot;Visual,&quot; and &quot;Financial&quot; truths, along with the CAC certificate. I will then:
(NEW) Use OCR to read the CAC certificate to extract the business name, registration number, and date of incorporation.
(NEW) Cross-check CAC data against the &quot;Stated Truth&quot; form data (e.g., does the company name match?) and the &quot;Financial Truth&quot; bank data (e.g., does the bank account name match the registered business name?).
Cross-check the stated financial information against the actual bank data from Mono.
Cross-check the stated business service (e.g., &quot;we sell shoes&quot;) against the &quot;Visual Truth&quot; video (e.g., &quot;video shows a shoe store&quot;).
Detect all inconsistencies or mismatches.
User Story 1.5 (System): As the PulseFi AI, I must generate a &quot;Pulse Score&quot; from 0-100 based on the consistency of all four data points (Form + CAC + Video + Bank Data). A mismatch in the CAC document will heavily penalize the score.
User Story 1.6 (Sade): As Sade, I must be able to see my Pulse Score on my dashboard and be notified if I have passed the minimum 75-point threshold. If I fail, I should see general reasons why (e.g., &quot;CAC document mismatch&quot;) so I can correct it if it&#39;s an honest mistake.
Epic 2: Profit Score Analysis (The &quot;Opportunity Zone&quot; )
User Story 2.1 (System): As the PulseFi AI, once an SME (Sade) is &quot;Pulse Verified&quot; (Score &gt; 75) , I must automatically conduct a deep analysis of their connected financial data.
User Story 2.2 (System): As the AI, I must analyze:
Profitability &amp; Cash Flow (revenue streams, expense patterns).
Growth Trajectory (revenue consistency, trends).
Customer Behavior (transaction patterns, retention).
User Story 2.3 (System): As the AI, I must use this analysis to generate a dynamic &quot;Profit Score&quot; that measures the SME&#39;s financial health and potential.
User Story 2.4 (Sade): As Sade, I should receive simple, AI-powered recommendations on how I can improve my financial health and score.
Epic 3: The Investor Marketplace
User Story 3.1 (Bayo): As Bayo (Lender), I must be able to log in to my dashboard and see a curated list of only 100% Pulse-Verified SMEs.
User Story 3.2 (Bayo): As Bayo, I must be able to filter these SMEs by key opportunities, such as Industry Sector, Geographic Location, and Profit Score Range.
User Story 3.3 (Bayo): As Bayo, when I click on a verified SME, I must be able to see their detailed profile, their Pulse Score (for authenticity), and their full Profit Score analysis (for opportunity).
